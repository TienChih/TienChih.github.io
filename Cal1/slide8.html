Product Rule

\[\frac{d}{dx}[f(x)g(x)]=f(x)g'(x)+f'(x)g(x). \]

Quotient Rule Rule

\[\frac{d}{dx}[\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}]=\frac{g(x)f'(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{g(x)^2}. \]

Let \(Q(x)=f(x)/g(x)\) \[\begin{array}{rcl} Q(x)g(x) & =& f(x)\\ \frac{d}{dx}[Q(x)g(x)] & =& f'(x)\\ Q'(x)g(x)+Q(x)g'(x) & =& f'(x)\\ Q'(x) & =& \frac{f'(x)-Q(x)g'(x)}{g(x)}\\ \frac{d}{dx}\left[ \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} \right]& =& \frac{f'(x)-\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}g'(x)}{g(x)}\\ & =& \frac{f'(x)\frac{g(x)}{g(x)}-\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}g'(x)}{g(x)}\\ & =& \frac{g(x)f'(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{g(x)^2}. \end{array}\]
