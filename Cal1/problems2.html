Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle \frac{1}{\sqrt{2x+2}}\), find the average rate of change of \(f(x)\) over \([1, 1+h]\).

Let \(g(t)=\displaystyle\frac{|t^2-9|}{t^2-4t+3}\), find \(\displaystyle \lim_{t\to 3^-}g(t), \lim_{t\to 3^+}g(t), \lim_{t\to 3}g(t).\)

Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle\frac{x+1}{(x-3)(x+2)^2}\), find \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\to -2^-}f(x), \lim_{x\to -2^+}f(x), \lim_{x\to -2}f(x).\)

Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle\frac{x+1}{(x-3)(x+2)^2}\), find \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\to 2^-}f(x), \lim_{x\to 2^+}f(x), \lim_{x\to 2}f(x).\)

Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle\frac{x+1}{(x-3)(x+2)^2}\), find \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\to 3^-}f(x), \lim_{x\to 3^+}f(x), \lim_{x\to 3}f(x).\)

Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle\frac{x+1}{(x-3)(x+2)^2}\), find \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\to -\infty}f(x), \lim_{x\to \infty}f(x).\)

Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle x\sin\left( \frac{1}{x^3}\right)\), find \(\displaystyle \lim_{x\to 0^+}f(x).\)

Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle \frac{1}{\sqrt{2x+2}}\), use the limit definition of the derivative to show that \(f'(1) = -\frac{1}{8}\).

Let \(f(x)=\displaystyle \frac{1}{\sqrt{2x+2}}\), find the line tangent to \(y=f(x)\) when \(x=1\).

Let \(g(x)=\displaystyle \frac{2}{x+3}\), use the limit definition of the derivative to show that \(g'(x) = -\frac{2}{(x+3)^2}\).
