Consider the differential \(d(uv)\). Which of the following are equivalent to \(d(uv)\)?

(A) \(dudv\ \ \ \) (B) \(du+dv\ \ \ \) (C) \(vdu+udv\ \ \ \) (D) \(udu+vdv\)

\[d(uv) = udv+vdu\]

\[\int d(uv) = \int (udv+vdu)\]

\[\int d(uv) = \int udv+\int vdu\]

\[ uv = \int udv+\int vdu\]

\[ \int udv = uv - \int vdu\]
