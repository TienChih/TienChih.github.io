How long does it take for the mass to decrease Deom \(A\) to \(\frac{A}{2}\)?

What's \(\Delta x\)?

\[\underline{R}\Delta t \leq \Delta x \leq \overline{R}\Delta t\]

\[\frac{\Delta x}{\overline{R}} \leq \Delta t \leq \frac{\Delta x}{\underline{R}}\]

\[\frac{A}{2\overline{R}} \leq \Delta t \leq \frac{A}{2\underline{R}}\]

\[\frac{A}{2\overline{R}} \leq \Delta t \leq \frac{A}{2\underline{R}}\]
