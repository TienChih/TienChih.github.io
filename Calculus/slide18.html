Implict differentiation

\[\begin{array}{rcl} x^2+y^2&=&16\\ \end{array}\]

\[\begin{array}{rcl} x^2+y^2&=&16\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2+y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ \end{array}\]

\[\begin{array}{rcl} x^2+y^2&=&16\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2+y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2]+\frac{d}{dx}[y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ \end{array}\]

\[\begin{array}{rcl} x^2+y^2&=&16\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2+y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2]+\frac{d}{dx}[y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ 2x\frac{d}{dx}[x]+2y\frac{d}{dx}[y]&=&0\\ \end{array}\]

\[\begin{array}{rcl} x^2+y^2&=&16\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2+y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2]+\frac{d}{dx}[y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ 2x\frac{d}{dx}[x]+2y\frac{d}{dx}[y]&=&0\\ 2x+2y\frac{dy}{dx}&=&0\\ \end{array}\]

\[\begin{array}{rcl} x^2+y^2&=&16\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2+y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ \frac{d}{dx}[x^2]+\frac{d}{dx}[y^2]&=&\frac{d}{dx}[16]\\ 2x\frac{d}{dx}[x]+2y\frac{d}{dx}[y]&=&0\\ 2x+2y\frac{dy}{dx}&=&0\\ \frac{dy}{dx}&=&-\frac{x}{y}.\\ \end{array}\]
