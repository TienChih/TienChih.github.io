\[\sin(\theta_2)-\sin(\theta_1) = 2\sin\left(\frac{\theta_2-\theta_1}{2}\right)\cos\left(\frac{\theta_2+\theta_1}{2}\right),\] \[\cos(\theta_2)-\cos(\theta_1) = -2\cos\left(\frac{\theta_2-\theta_1}{2}\right)\sin\left(\frac{\theta_2+\theta_1}{2}\right).\]

\[2\sin\left(\frac{\theta_2-\theta_1}{2}\right)\cos\left(\frac{\theta_2+\theta_1}{2}\right)=\sin(\theta_2)-\sin(\theta_1) = 2\sin\left(\frac{\theta_2-\theta_1}{2}\right)\cos\left(\frac{\theta_2+\theta_1}{2}\right)\]
