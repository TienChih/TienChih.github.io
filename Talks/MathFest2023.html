About Me!

  • I am an Assistant Professor at Oxford College of Emory University.

  • I have been heavily involved with the Team Based Inquiry Learning (TBIL) project.

About Oxford College

  • Liberal Arts experience for 1st and 2nd year Emory students.
  • Students are relatively well-prepared and high achieving.
  • Multivariable Calculus is typically full (25 students).
  • 3 credit hours, includes some proofs, and goes up to Gauss's Theorem.

What is TBIL?



  • A way to include inquiry learning into lower division courses.
  • Students work in teams on scaffolded activities.
  • There is oppurtunity for in group and whole class discussions.
  • There is material for Calc 1 & 2, Linear Algebra and Intro Stats.
